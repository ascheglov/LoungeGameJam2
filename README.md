# Sheep and Cabbage

*~ The 2nd Great Lounge Game Jam ~*

## Story and gameplay

On a small island a **herd** of sheep is trying to **escape** from a fenced area and eat cabbage.

You have to **repair** fence to prevent them from escaping.
